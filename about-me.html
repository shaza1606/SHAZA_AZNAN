My Life Advice

Below is what I wish I understand at early life.

Your relationship with others is a direct reflection of your relationship with yourself. If you treat yourself poorly, you unconsciously seek out and tolerate others who mistreat you as well. And if you treat yourself with dignity and respect, it's vice versa.

The only way to feel better about yourself is to do things that are worth feeling good about. Respect is earned, not given.

You give power to who you blame. If you blame someone for your problems, you unconsciously give them power over you by allowing them to dictate your happiness and well-being.

Life is short, so spend it wisely and treasure your loved ones. You will never know when will be your last time to see them.

Motivation isn't the cause of action, but the effect. Force yourself to carry on, then let momentum carry you forward.

A happy life isn't a life without stress, it's a life of meaningful stress. Also, everything comes with inherent sacrifice to it. Whatever makes us feel good, also inevitably makes us feel bad.

If you are afraid to say "no", your "yes" means nothing. You are just a reflection of the preferences and demands of people around you. If you don't decide who you are, others will decide it for you.

Trust is the currency of all relationships. Every failed relationship is mainly caused by broken trust. Honesty and integrity are significant in relationships and happiness.
