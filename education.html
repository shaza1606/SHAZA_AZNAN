Experiences

During the final phase of my diploma, I completed a two-month internship as a librarian. This invaluable experience significantly enriched my knowledge and deepened my understanding of my field study. I not only sharpened my social skills and fostered strong relationships with colleagues but also undertook significant projects that enhanced my understanding of library operations. One notable achievement was handling a special project to ensure accurate and visually compelling information displays throughout the library, which underscored my attention to detail and commitment to improving visitor experiences. Moreover, I gained profound insights into library systems—from cataloging and archival management to reader services—deepening my appreciation for how libraries function to serve their communities effectively.
